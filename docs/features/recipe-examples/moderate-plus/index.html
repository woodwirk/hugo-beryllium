03 - Moderate+ - Zucchini Bread

2024-01-01
breakfast

Ingredients

Equipment
  • 2x 900g bread tin (4x8")
Loaf
  • All-Purpose Flour: 3 cups
  • Salt: 1 tsp
  • Baking Soda: 1 tsp
  • Baking Powder: 1 tsp
  • Ground Cinnamon: 1 tbsp
  • Eggs: 3
  • Vegetable Oil: 1 cup
  • White Sugar: 2 1/4 cups
  • Vanilla Extract: 3 tsp
  • Zucchini (Grated): 2 cups (1 zucchini)
  • Walnuts (Chopped): 1 cup
  1. Preheat oven to 325 F
  2. Grease and flour two bread tins
  3. Beat eggs, oil, vanilla, and sugar in a large bowl
  4. Mix flour, salt, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon separately
  5. Add granular ingredients to large bowl, little by little, and mix well
  6. Stir in zucchini and nuts
  7. Pour batter into pans and bake 40-60 minutes
  8. Cool for 20 minutes in pan, on rack
